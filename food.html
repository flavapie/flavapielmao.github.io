Food

This page will feature about my likes and dislikes in food.

The picture shown on the left is Sinigang na Baboy. It is my favorite dish. Other than the delicious soft pork meat, the broth is what makes it taste the best. Mixed together with tomatoes and other vegetables to make its flavor.

Truly a magnificent dish. It is something I think every Filipino should try at least once in their lifetimes.

Tinola

Tinola was something my mom always cooked here at home. It's one of my favorite dishes because of the great texture and taste of the chicken. I would not also forget how the soup enhances the whole dish itself. The vegetables add a better taste.Also something that people should give a try.

Likes and Dislikes

Likes

I like to eat sour things. Sour food is really great, they stimualte my taste and the feeling I get is impeccable. Sour foods or snacks like Sinigang, sour candy, or lemonade.

I also like fruits, especially oranges and tangerines. It's really refreshing especially when they're served cold and fresh from the fridge. Well, at least I eat the common fruits such as apples, bananas, and other things.

Dislikes

I dislike bitter dishes. This includes ampalaya. If I eat something as bitter as that, I would gag. Other than bitter food, I also dislike sweet dishes.

There's a diference between sweet snacks and sweet dishes. I'm alright with sweet snacks but not sweet dishes. One dish, Pata tim, was served to me one time. After that, I never wanted to eat sweet dishes ever again. I thought it was adobo.
