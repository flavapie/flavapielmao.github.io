Anime

My Top 5 Anime


1) Working!

Working! is one of my first anime series that I watched. It really engraved a deep sense of love for anime within me. A romcom story about employees working at a family restaurant. Something I very much appreciate.





2) Violet Evergarden

A great story featuring a woman whose name is Violet Evergarden. A story that showed sadness, joy, and even romance. I had a great time watching it and I would recommend it to anybody.


3) A Silent Voice

A story that hints depression. Watching it will really hit you in the feels. The theme it gives is really amazing and it actually made me cry. Truly a must watch among anime-watchers.



4) Mob Psycho 100

Mob Pyscho 100 is an action/psychological anime. Though people watch it for the great fight scenes, the character development is actually a great story in itself. It features a simple boy, who goes by Mob, who lives on to experience new emotions. Plus, there's a season 3 announced!!. Overall, a really good watch.


5) Tanaka-kun is Always Listless

A funny comedy anime about a listless guy, Tanaka. He tries to survive everyday from the tiring activities that come his way. Perhaps his listlessness is helping him, or is it causing him more trouble. A great watch for anyone who wants to get started in anime.
